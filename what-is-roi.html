What Is ROI?

ROI (Return on Investment) is a financial metric used to measure the profitability of an investment. It helps businesses and investors evaluate how efficiently their capital is being used.

ROI Formula

The ROI formula is:
ROI = (Net Profit / Investment Cost) Ã— 100%

Why ROI Is Important

  • Measures business performance
  • Helps compare different investments
  • Used in marketing, startups, real estate

ROI Example

If you invest $1,000 and earn $1,300, your net profit is $300. Your ROI would be 30%.
